A New Friend ü§ù

Then he reached class and got shocked,
Due to sudden turns of events his mind was blocked.

Hope that he found today was in his classroom,
Couldn't even say hi even in the same room.

He was regretting his decision of not saying hi,
The next moment she sat next to him and asked why.

It's not that easy to ask,
And for me it's an impossible task.

You overthink too much, you silly,
We are friends from now on, meet me here daily.

Is it a dream or a beautiful day?
He wondered softly along the way.

Maybe his loneliness had found an end,
That's how they became each other's first friend.
