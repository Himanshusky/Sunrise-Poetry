Chapter 3

The Sketch 👀

First bell rings and the lecture starts,
No lecture today we will learn art.

After hearing art both of their face change,
She was happy and he was acting strange.

She gets the situation and ask calmly - are you not good in art,
He replied with hesitation - me and art are very apart.

Sketching things is an boring task,
Expressing your emotions and thoughts on paper is never just an task.

She replied-Art is never good and bad,
It' s the observer who can even give lives to dead.

then she look for her sketch book in bag,
He understands about Art but his brain lag.

Showing him sketches and telling the stories,
They both are smiling and forget about worries.

Draw something that comes in your mind,
Everyone started sketching and lost in their mind.

Portraying their emotions and everyone grind,
after sometimes he started peeking from behind.

Did you completed your sketch he asks,
Replied in teasing tone- just finished the task.

She looked at his desk and get shocked,
Draw two students in rain near that block.(the area where they both met).

She was smiling and blushing hard,
You should tell me before about this special card(his sketching skills).

Then he asks with a curious smile,
show me yours i am waiting for a while.
