Chapter 06

Now they both reached her home with excitement,
He was nervous and froze in that very moment.

Then she comforted him and rang the bell,
By looking into his eyes, she knew he was not well.

She held his hand, saying everything will be fine,
He forgot to worry and enjoyed the divine.

Then her mother opened the door,
Greeted them politely and started smiling more.

“Are you her classmate, am I right?”
He felt delighted hearing she spoke about him with pride.

“You both sit here, I am bringing some snacks,”
“Let me show you my room once I came back.”

“Don’t move from here, or I’ll kill you with this knife,”
Then she left to change her clothes, full of life.

He quietly obeyed her order,
Then she came back and offered him water.

With a big smile, he looked at a picture on the wall,
A cute little girl with an adorable smile playing with a ball.

“That’s you, I’m pretty sure,” he said with confidence,
“How did you know?” she asked with elegance.

“Clearly, because your eyes are still that beautiful,
As she smiled he said "it's still so cheerful".”

Then her mother brought the snacks,
They enjoyed them, and he said, “I need to go back.”

“At least wait to meet her dad,” her mom said,
“I need to go, else my mom will be mad.”

Then his phone rings again, and he started to worry,
“Okay, go home safely, no need to hurry.”

“Thanks for everything, I really enjoyed the evening,
Next time I’ll meet uncle, that’s a sure thing.”

Then she walked him outside and said,
“Now go home, idiot, and call me when you reached.”

— Sunrise Poetry
