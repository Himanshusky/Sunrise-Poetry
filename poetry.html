Chapter 1

A ray of Hope✨

An introvert boy who is all alone,
Not good in study always have depressed tone.

Same school since childhood still no friend,
To his loneliness and bad luck there is no end.

There is a new admission in school,
A caring girl who is gentle and cool.

They met each other due to the rain,
In first encounter he thought she really is beauty with brain.

Stop staring at me that's what she said,
Not staring at you I just love rain idiot.

Are you always that rude and depressed,
Is your feeling and emotions that much suppressed.

Then for the first time he smiles,
In a gentle tone sorry for mess - he leaves.

He just found his new hope today,
Someone who understands him without spending a day.
